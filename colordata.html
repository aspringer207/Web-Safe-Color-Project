Web Safe Colors

Welcome! I hope this color chart winds up being useful to you. My goal for this project has been to better understand hex codes, and become familiar enough to plug in hex values without having to rely on a color picker. I decided that using the 216 web-safe hex colors was a good way to start. That turned out to be a good instinct. I also decided to forego a tutorial and wander into an empty spreadsheet demanding that it reveal to me the secrets of the hexadecimals. That did not turn out to be a good instinct. Nevertheless, I know more than I knew when I started, which was the goal. On this page you'll find a few links with some tips for moving beyond a CTRL C, CTRL V relationship with hex colors.
